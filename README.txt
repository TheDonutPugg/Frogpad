This original version is a simple wired USB keypad made for writing equations in the MS word equation editor. it has 9 buttons, 3 of which are non-stanard on a regular keyboard. It has open Parentheses, closed parentheses, plus, minus, forward slash, asterisk, square root, superscript, and subscript. Though for the subscript key, a bind must be manually made in microsoft word. The whole thing is controlled by an arduino micro and the top piece is designed for cherry MX keyswitches. The top and bottom pieces are made to be held together with number 6 machine screws.
